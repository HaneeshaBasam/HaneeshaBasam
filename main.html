Who We Are

We are a team of passionate researchers, data scientists, and AI enthusiasts dedicated to advancing environmental monitoring through artificial intelligence. Leveraging the power of crowd-sourced data and state-of-the-art AI techniques, we aim to enhance the resolution and accuracy of satellite images, turning them into actionable insights for decision-makers, environmental agencies, and the public.

What We Do

Our project focuses on downscaling low-resolution satellite images using machine learning algorithms, enriched by crowd-sourced data. By incorporating real-time ground-level inputs from users worldwide, our AI models can refine and enhance satellite imagery, providing a clearer, more precise picture of air quality at any given moment. This collaborative approach not only improves the accuracy of our models but also empowers communities to participate in environmental monitoring.
